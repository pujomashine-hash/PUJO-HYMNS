 TWENDE KWA YESU 

 
1.Twende kwa Yesu mimi nawe,
  Njia atwonya tuijue
  Imo Chuoni; na Mwenyewe,
  Hapa asema, Njoo!
 
//Na furaha tutaiona,
  Mioyo ikitakata sana,
  Kwako, Mwokozi, kuonana,
  Na milele kukaa.
 
2.”Wana na waje”, atwambia,
   Furahini mkisikia,
   Ndiye mfalme wetu pia,
   Na tumtii, Njoo.
//Na furaha tuationa......
 
3.Wangojeani? Leo yupo;
  Sikiza sana asemapo;
  Huruma zake zikiwapo,
  Ewe kijana, Njoo.
//Na furaha tuationa .......
 
Alternative: Tenzi 38.
English Hymn: Come to the savior,make no delay.
