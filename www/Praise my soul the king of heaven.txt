  𝙋𝙍𝘼𝙄𝙎𝙀 𝙈𝙔 𝙎𝙊𝙐𝙇 𝙏𝙃𝙀 𝙆𝙄𝙉𝙂 𝙊𝙁 𝙃𝙀𝘼𝙑𝙀𝙉 
Author: Henry Francis Lyte

1.Praise, my soul, 
  the King of heaven;
  to his feet your
  tribute bring.
  Ransomed, healed,
  restored, forgiven,
  evermore his praises sing.
  Alleluia, alleluia!
  Praise the everlasting King!

2.Praise him for his
  grace and favor
  to his people in distress.
  Praise him, still
  the same as ever,
  slow to chide, 
  and swift to bless.
  Alleluia, alleluia!
  Glorious in his faithfulness!

3.Fatherlike he tends
  and spares us;
  well our feeble 
  frame he knows.
  In his hand he 
  gently bears us,
  rescues us from all our foes.
  Alleluia, alleluia!
  Widely yet his mercy flows!

4.Angels, help us 
  to adore him;
  you behold him 
  face to face.
  Sun and moon, bow
  down before him,
  dwellers all in
  time and space.
  Alleluia, alleluia!
  Praise with us the God of grace!
