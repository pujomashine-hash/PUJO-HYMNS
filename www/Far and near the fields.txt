  𝙁𝘼𝙍 𝘼𝙉𝘿 𝙉𝙀𝘼𝙍 𝙏𝙃𝙀 𝙁𝙄𝙀𝙇𝘿𝙎
Author:	James Oren Thompson (1885)

1.Far and near the fields are teeming
  with the waves of ripened grain;
  far and near their gold is gleaming
  o'er the sunny slope and plain.

Refrain:
Lord of harvest,
send forth reapers;
hear us, Lord,
to thee we cry;
send them now the 
sheaves to gather,
ere the harvest-time pass by.

2.Send them forth with 
  morn's first beaming,
  send them in the noontide's glare;
  when the sun's last
  rays are gleaming,
  bid them gather ev'rywhere. 
  [Refrain]

3.Thou whom Christ 
  the Lord is sending,
  gather now the sheaves of gold;
  heav'nward then at
  evening wending,
  thou shalt come 
  with joy untold. 
  [Refrain]
