𝒀𝑬 𝑴𝑼𝑺𝑻 𝑩𝑬 𝑩𝑶𝑹𝑵 𝑨𝑮𝑨𝑰𝑵 
𝐴𝑢𝑡ℎ𝑜𝑟:	𝑊𝑖𝑙𝑙𝑖𝑎𝑚 𝑇. 𝑆𝑙𝑒𝑒𝑝𝑒𝑟 (1877)

1.A ruler once harkened
  to Jesus by night
  To ask him the way of
  salvation and light;
  The master made answer in
  words true and plain:
  You must be born again.

Refrain:
You must be born again,
You must be born again,
I verily, verily say unto you,
You must be born again.

2.O children of frailty,
  attend to the word
  So solemnly uttered 
  by Jesus, the Lord,
  And let not this message
   to you be in vain:
  You must be born again.
 [Refrain]

3.O ye who would enter
  that glorious rest,
  And sing with the ransomed
  the song of the blest,
  The life everlasting
   if you would obtain,
  You must be born again.
 [Refrain]

4.A dear one departed your
  heart yearns to see,
  At Heaven's brite gate 
  may be watching for thee,
  So list to the note of
  this solemn refrain:
  You must be born again.
 [Refrain]
