CHRIST IS ARISEN
Text: German (12th Century) / Martin Luther (1524)
Translation: W. Gustave Polack (1941)

1. Christ is arisen
   From the grave's dark prison.
   We now rejoice with gladness;
   Christ will solace all our sadness.
   Kyrieleis.

2. Had He not arisen,
   In death's gloomy prison
   We all would be forsaken;
   But now is the world awaken.
   Kyrieleis.

3. Alleluia, 
   Alleluia, 
   Alleluia!
   We now rejoice with gladness;
   Christ will solace all our sadness.
   Kyrieleis.
