 YESU AWAKUBALI WAKOSA

 
1.Yesu awakubali,
  Wakosa, wahalifu,
  Wambieni wa mbali
  Habari ya wokovu. 
 
//Tangazeni kwa bidii,
  “Akubali wakosa!”
  Liwe neno dhahiri,
  “Akubali wakosa!” 
 
2.Awakubali Bwana
  Neno lake amini
  Watu kila aina
  Waje kwake tengoni.
//Tangazeni kwa bidii...
 
3.Mimi ni safi moyo
  Na mbele ya sharia,
  Aliye safi roho
  Kwake ilitimia.
//Tangazeni kwa bidii......
 
4.Akubali wakosa;
  Nami anikubali;
  Alivyonitakasa
  Mbinguni niwasili.
//Tangazeni kwa bidii......
 
English Hymn: Sinners, Jesus will receive 
Alternative: Tenzi 32.
