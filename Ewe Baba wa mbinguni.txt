 EWE BABA WA MBINGUNI 
 
1.Ewe, Baba wa Mbinguni,
  Usikie twombalo
  Hapa chini duniani,
  Na uwe radhi nalo
 
//Ee Mungu, bariki
  Ndugu hawa wawili!
  Ee Mungu, bariki
  Ndugu hawa wawili!
 
2.Watu hawa mbele zako,
  Na mbele ya kanisa;
  Watimize Neno lako
  Wanakutana sasa.
//Ee Mungu bariki........
 
3.Kama Yesu na Kanisa,
  Ni mmoja, na hivyo,
  Watu hawawape sasa
  Wawe mmoja vivyo
//Ee Mungu bariki...........
 
4.Walinde, Bwana, daima,
  Wabariki nyumbani
  Uwape nyingi salama
  Humu ulimwenguni.
//Ee Mungu bariki...........
 
5.Wabariki, ewe Bwana,
  Watu hawa wawili,
  Wape upaji wa wana,
  Wafurahishwe kweli.
//Ee Mungu bariki............
 
6.Siku za duniani
  Zitakapopungua,
  Wape kurithi Mbinguni
  Mema ya kwendelea.
//Ee Mungu bariki.............
