  PELELEZA NDANI YANGU 
 
1.Peleleza ndani yangu,
  Iwe safi nia,
  Kwangu kama kwako Mungu,
  Idhihiri pia. 
 
2.Peleleza moyo wangu
  Uunifunulie,
  Yaliyomo ndani yangu
  Nami niyajue.
 
3.Kwanza washe zako tambi,
  Kumefunga giza;
  Nijue ambavyo dhambi
  Ni la kuchukiza.
 
4.Peleleza na mawazo
  Ni mbegu za mambo,
  Asili ya machukizo,
  Maumbuo – umbo.
 
5.Zidi kuyapeleleza
  Katikati yangu
  Hata wishe nifundisha
  Udhaifu wangu.
 
6.Hapo nikikwinamia
  Mbele zako, Mungu,
  Hakika nitakujua
  U mpenzi wangu.
