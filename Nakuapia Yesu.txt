            NAKUAPIA YESU

1.Nakuapia yesu,kukutumikia,
  wewe u mfunzi wangu,
  na rafiki mwema,
  sita ogopa vita,nikikufuata,
  sipotezi njia ,ukiniongoza.

2.Dunia ikaribu,Bwana unilinde,
  ma majaribu mengi, yapo pande zote 
  adui siku zote,ni ndani na nje,
  ee Yesu univute, karibu na wewe.
 
3.Nazisikia sauti, zenye tamaa mbaya,
  na mambo ya dunia, yanivuta sana,
  ee Bwana sema nami, furaha iwepo,
  nitakusikiliza, wewe mlinzi wangu.

4.Waagana na wote, wakufuatao,
  kwamba uliko wewe, nao watakuwa,
  nami ninaagana, kukutumikia,
  yesu nisaidie, kukuandamia.

5.Hatua zako Bwana, naziona wazi,
  Nizikanyage vyema, hapa duniani,
  nishike hata mwisho, ee Yesu mwokozi,
  nichukue nikifa, na kwako Mbinguni..
